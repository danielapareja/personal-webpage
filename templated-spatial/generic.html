Introduction

This course is aimed at learning how to model, analyse and solve complex decision making problems with linear programming, that appear in management or design of industrial systems like transportation and distribution networks, manufacturing products, logistics, amongst others.

It is a core course for any Industrial Engineer and therefore it is very important to learn and understand profoundly. This webpage is aimed not only at introducing me but also to present the Laboratory Challenges that are presented to me and my fellow classmates during my DMO classes at UTEC.
